# Message to share with coaches

Short, friendly, leads with why I actually stopped using the page — not a feature list.

---

Hey — wanted to share a little thing I've been working on.

I've been four months into the custom program and barely doing it, and it wasn't because I didn't want to. The page itself kept tripping me up:

- Videos killed my music or Audible the moment they loaded, even though they have no audio I actually needed.
- My screen would go to sleep between sets. When I came back, the page had usually reloaded (losing my spot), or the videos had stopped working and I'd have to refresh anyway — which also dumped me at the top.
- It's all on one endless page, so losing my spot meant scrolling through 5 days of exercises to find where I was.
- Tapping a video to go fullscreen kicked me out of my spot too.
- Videos didn't always loop, so I'd have to manually restart them while holding a position.
- On patchy gym wifi, videos just… didn't load.

All together, the page was annoying enough that the whole program just didn't happen for me.

I built a little phone app that fixes exactly those things: videos start muted so my audio keeps playing, each day is its own screen, fullscreen doesn't lose my place, it works offline, the screen doesn't sleep mid-workout. I also threw in some extras — set logging, rest and hold timers, a streak, one-tap form-check to WhatsApp — but honestly those are bonuses. The real win is I actually open it every day now.
