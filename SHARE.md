# Message to share with coaches

Short, friendly, leads with why I actually stopped using the page — not a feature list.

---

Hey — wanted to share a little thing I've been working on.

I've been four months into the custom program and barely doing it (I'd do some exercises on some days and all those were loaded into FitBod), and it wasn't because I didn't want to. The page itself kept tripping me up:

- Videos killed my music or Audible the moment they loaded, even though they have no audio I actually needed. And I NEED my audio... 
- My screen would go to sleep between exercises. When I came back, the page had usually reloaded (losing my spot), or the videos had stopped working and I'd have to refresh anyway — which also dumped me at the top.
- It's all on one endless page, so losing my spot meant scrolling through all the days of exercises to find where I was.
- Tapping a video to go fullscreen kicked me out of my spot too.
- Videos didn't always loop, so I'd have to manually restart them while trying to do something new... which also sometimes caused the page to reload. 
- On patchy wifi, videos just don't load.

All together, the page was annoying enough that the whole program just didn't happen for me... at least not the way it was meant to. And I love this program. I love the coaching and I love the training. But I hate the Wordpress page. 

Then I got an updated program (2 full new days!!!) and I spent a whole week not doing a single new exercise because the whole custom page really bothers me that much (i know, i know)... 

So...I built a super simple little phone app that fixes exactly those things: videos start muted so my audio keeps playing, each day is its own screen, fullscreen doesn't lose my place, and the screen doesn't sleep mid-workout. The app itself (navigation, logging, timers) works offline; videos still need a connection because they stream from Vimeo. I also threw in some extras — set logging, rest and hold timers, a streak (local tracking only right now), one-tap form-check to WhatsApp — but honestly those are bonuses. The real win is I actually open it every day now.
